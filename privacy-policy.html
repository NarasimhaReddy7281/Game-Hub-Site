1. Introduction

Welcome to Game Hub, a mobile gaming platform featuring multiple mini games, available on Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use our app, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. By using Game Hub, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The following non-personal data may be collected automatically, primarily through third-party advertising and analytics services:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage and interaction data (e.g., which games are played)
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (country/region level, for ad targeting only)
  • Crash reports and performance data

This data is collected in aggregate and cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. How We Use Information

Any data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Displaying relevant advertisements within the app

  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or rent any user data to third parties.

4. Permissions Used

Game Hub may request the following permissions on your Android device:

  • Internet Access (INTERNET) – Required to load advertisements and enable online features within the app.
  • Network State (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE) – Used to check connectivity before loading ads or online content.

No permissions are used to access your camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or location beyond what is listed above.
